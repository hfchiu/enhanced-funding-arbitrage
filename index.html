關於我們 About Us

油尖旺體育會成立於1985年，是香港一個致力於推廣體育運動的非牟利組織。我們的使命是透過體育活動促進社區健康，培養青少年的體育精神和團隊合作能力。

多年來，我們一直秉承「健康、團結、進步」的宗旨，為油尖旺區居民提供優質的體育服務。我們的活動範圍涵蓋多個體育項目，包括足球、籃球、羽毛球、乒乓球、游泳等。

我們相信體育運動不僅能強身健體，更能培養品格，建立友誼，促進社區和諧。歡迎各界人士加入我們的大家庭！

Yau Tsim Mong Sports Association was established in 1985 as a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sports in Hong Kong. Our mission is to foster community health through sports activities and cultivate sportsmanship and teamwork among young people.

Over the years, we have adhered to our motto of "Health, Unity, Progress," providing quality sports services to residents of the Yau Tsim Mong district. Our activities cover various sports including football, basketball, badminton, table tennis, swimming, and more.

We believe that sports not only strengthen the body but also build character, forge friendships, and promote community harmony. We welcome people from all walks of life to join our big family!
